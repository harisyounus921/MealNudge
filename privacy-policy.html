Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

MealNudge respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

MealNudge may collect information you provide inside the app, including your profile details, health goals, meal logs, activity logs, preferences, and app usage data.

How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Track meals, calories, macros, and activity
  • Calculate nutrition goals and progress
  • Provide diet coaching and personalized insights
  • Improve app features and user experience

Data Storage

MealNudge currently stores app data locally on your device. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

Future versions of MealNudge may use third-party services for nutrition data, health integrations, analytics, authentication, or AI coaching. These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

Health Information

MealNudge is designed for general wellness and nutrition tracking. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. Please consult a qualified healthcare professional before making major diet, fitness, or health decisions.

Data Export and Deletion

MealNudge may allow you to export your data or delete stored app data from the settings screen. Deleting data may remove meal logs, activity logs, profile details, and chat history from the app.

Children's Privacy

MealNudge is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
