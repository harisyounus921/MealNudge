Data and Privacy

MealNudge stores app data locally on your device in the current version. You can manage your data from the app settings, including exporting or deleting stored app data.

Medical Disclaimer

MealNudge is intended for general wellness and nutrition tracking only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Please consult a qualified healthcare professional before making major diet, fitness, or health decisions.
